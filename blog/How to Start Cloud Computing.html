Simply put, cloud computing is similar or identical to the delivery of computing services. Now, computing services include servers, databases, storage ,software, networking, analytics, and intelligence. Basically, anything over the Internet (‚Äúthe cloud‚Äù) to offer faster innovation, flexible resources, and economies of scale. You typically pay only for cloud services you use, helping lower your operating costs, run your infrastructure more efficiently and scale as your business needs change. So, in short, we use cloud computing to lower the storage costs that would have otherwise been used on hardware devices to store the immaculate data that comes with any company, business or even a startup. Now, answering the main question, ‚ÄúHow to start Cloud Computing on Prepinsta‚Äù, you need to know that there are certain skills and subjects you should know prior to your learning of the course. If you have a grasp over them , you are good to go with a professional certificate in Cloud computing. They are:-
  • Programming Skills
  • Familiarity with Databases
  • Basics of Security and Privacy
  • Knowledge of Agile Development
  • Familiarity with Operating Systems
  • Understanding of Virtualization
  • Basics of Networking


Once you are familiar with the terms enough to explain them to a five year old, you can get started with your Cloud Computing course on Prepinsta, just like I did.

Here is what PrepInsta offers in the Cloud Computing Course:-

  • A Basic 1 hour Introduction to Cloud Computing which will clear the basics of a students who wish to learn Cloud Computing and are even from Non-Technical backgrounds.
  • Next is a 2+ hour section which explains Why you should begin with Cloud Computing aka its advantages and disadvantages
  • AWS Instances makes the next part of the module which covers the three sections: Launching an instance, What is an AMI, Getting a free domain
