Roadmap To Start Your Placement Preparation

Decemeber 21, 2022
To begin with, the first thing that every student should know is that there are three types of companies in India. In this map, we are going to explain in detail the Roadmap To Start Your Placement Preparations. Service-based Companies: Companies that provide services to other companies or clients. For Example, TCS, Wipro, Cognizant, and many more. Product-based Companies: Companies that launch new products in the market. For Example, Facebook, Google, Microsoft, and many more. Startups: Companies that work on a micro-scale are in their nascent stage. There are different strategies for different types of companies. Let‚Äôs first look at how to get into a service-based company.

  1. Step 1: Coding

    2. Pick any one language from: Python, C, CPP, Java Then go to DSA in that language DSA in python, DSA in CPP (this includes DSA in C), DSA in Java. At last, you can start working on some competitive programming questions:

    • Basic Coding
    • Intermediate Coding
    • Competitive Coding


  2. Step 2: Aptitude

    There will be different sections in aptitude. They are mentioned as follows:
    • Logical
    • Verbal
    • Aptitude


    Each section is equally important to score marks and get selected for the next stage of the Job hiring. So always focus on all concepts.

  3. Step 3: Interviews

    In a Service-based company, there are two stages in an Interview, they are:

    • Technical
    • HR


    TR Interview is conducted first. In TR(Technical Round), the Interviewer will ask questions based on your job role. It can be about coding languages, logical questions, programming questions, and other core subject questions like SQL, DBMS, Networks and so on. If one clears this round, your chances of getting a Job Offer are 90%. It would be best if you were thorough with the language you have been learning, along with the concepts of Data Structures. Also, it would be best if you focused on the skill requirement sections in the Job Description to check for core subjects, then prepare only for specific topics/subjects. The HR Interview is the final round for getting a selection. In the HR Interview, they will ask you about your basic profile details. They will test your communication skills by giving you some scenarios on topics like Team Management, client dealing and so forth. Finally, the Interviewer will share the details of the job role. At last, you will receive a Congratulatory Mail from the company.
